The economics
don't extract.

  • No fees on backers.Withdraw anytime, principal-intact. No exit fees, no carry, no "performance" cuts.
  • No fees on founders.Zero platform cut when they raise. Round goes to them, full stop.
  • No platform token.Ever. Kommits aren't tradeable. The unit can't be airdrop bait.
  • Audited yield funds it.Capital sits in Kamino ($3B TVL). Interest covers operations. Same math as PoolTogether.
Legal precedent

Kent v. PoolTogether — dismissed.

Principal-protected pooled-yield products are not securities under U.S. law. The legal path is cleared. We sit on the right side of the precedent.
