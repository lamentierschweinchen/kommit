What Kommit is 03 / 10

An upvote
with a stake.

Park your money. Allocate it across early-stage teams you want to back. The yield your deposit earns funds the platform — no fees on either side. Withdraw anytime; your principal stays yours.

What you earn is kommits — capital × time, soulbound, public, verifiable. A portable record of conviction.

→Right of first allocation when a backed team raises its public round. →Access and perks — beta keys, kommitter-only updates, cohort events. →Portable across products. Any SaaS, hiring platform, or partner can read kommit balances with no permission.

Why now 06 / 10

Three shifts
made it possible.

01
Yield funds the platform. Audited yield protocols (Aave, Morpho, Kamino) now clear retail-scale deposits. Yield on idle capital covers ops entirely — zero fees on either side.
02
Walletless onboarding works. Privy + passkeys + email auth. A backer signs up in under 30 seconds and never sees the word "crypto."
03
The regulatory question got answered. Dismissal of Kent v. PoolTogether signaled that principal-protected yield products are not unregistered securities — the exact model Kommit uses.

For the first time, you can back a team without paying for the privilege or losing your principal.
